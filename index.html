Data Science Capstone Project

In this module, students carry out a Data Science Capstone project that brings together the theoretical modules from the first year of the MIT Big Data Science program. The course involves a blend of lectures and practical group assignments, culminating in a capstone project presentation.

This website serves as a public platform to share information about the course, student projects, and recommended readings. For enrolled University of Pretoria students, official course information and materials are available on ClickUP.

Since 2020, this class has been taught by Prof. Vukosi Marivate and Dr. Abiodun Modupe.
