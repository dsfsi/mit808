Data Science Capstone Project

In this module students will carry out a Data Science Capstone project that brings together the theoretical modules you would have done in the first year of your MIT C Big Data Science program. We will be somewhat following the Epicycles of Analysis from the Art of Data Science.

Note: This is not an official course website. This is an informational site to share some resources for other people who might be interested in resources and research in this area. For UP students who have enrolled on this class information should be accessed through ClickUP!!!

Since 2020, this class has been taught by Prof. Vukosi Marivate and Dr. Abiodun Modupe.
